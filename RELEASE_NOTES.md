# FrameScript v0.3.0

FrameScript v0.3.0 adds focused AI assistance and makes everyday editing more dependable:

- Write with inline AI autocomplete at the end of a script: review one-sentence ghost text, accept it with Tab, or dismiss it with Escape.
- Connect Google AI Studio alongside OpenAI-compatible endpoints, OpenRouter, and Groq.
- Remove individual Recent Projects or clear the list without deleting project files.
- Work with consistent Script, Visuals, and Editing terminology throughout the app and exports.
- Get clearer, localized recovery for project, export, Keychain, Recent Project, settings, and AI failures.
- Keep text, caret, selection, scroll position, live metrics, production markers, and saved-project autosave more reliable across editing and window transitions.

FrameScript writes `.fscr` project format version 3, reads versions 1–3, and still imports legacy `.framescript` files. Inline review markers are not yet shown in the editor, and production anchors are not guaranteed to survive arbitrary rewrites of linked text.
